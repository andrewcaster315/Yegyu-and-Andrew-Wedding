How it happened우리의 만남

Our Story우리의 이야기

It started with a birthday. On September 16, 2025, Yegyu was visiting New York City with her mom and aunt to celebrate — and somehow, in a city of eight million people, she crossed paths with Andrew.

That same evening became their first date at a wine bar in the city. The next day she joined him at a Mets game. That Friday she came to his best friend Mike's birthday party. And somehow, after all of that, neither of them wanted it to stop.

What began as an unexpected birthday encounter turned into something neither of them planned and both of them treasure. On September 19th, 2026 — in Seoul, the city Yegyu calls home — we'll celebrate that beginning together.

— Andrew & Yegyu

모든 것은 생일에서 시작되었습니다. 2025년 9월 16일, 예규는 생일을 맞아 엄마, 이모와 함께 뉴욕을 여행하고 있었습니다. 그리고 800만 명의 도시에서, 운명처럼 앤드류를 만났습니다.

그날 저녁 와인 바에서의 첫 데이트를 시작으로, 다음 날에는 메츠 야구 경기를 함께 관람했고, 그 주 금요일에는 앤드류의 절친한 친구 Mike의 생일 파티에도 함께했습니다. 그렇게 이어진 만남은 멈출 수가 없었습니다.

우연한 생일의 만남이 두 사람 모두 소중히 여기는 인연이 되었습니다. 2026년 9월 19일, 예규의 고향 서울에서 그 특별한 시작을 함께 축하합니다.

— 앤드류 & 예규

Q & A

US citizens can visit South Korea visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism. Your passport just needs to be valid for the duration of your stay — no advance application required. You'll receive an entry stamp on arrival at Incheon.

미국 시민권자는 관광 목적으로 최대 90일간 무비자 입국이 가능합니다.

AREX Express Train — the easiest option. About 43 minutes to Seoul Station for around $10 USD. Follow "Airport Railroad" signs right after customs. Highly recommended.

Taxi — great if you have a lot of luggage. Around $50–70 USD and 60–90 min depending on traffic. Use the official taxi stand outside arrivals — avoid anyone soliciting rides inside the terminal.

공항철도(AREX)로 서울역까지 약 43분, 택시로 약 60~90분 소요됩니다.

Yes — strongly recommended. Don't rely on your US carrier plan. An eSIM is the easiest solution — buy and activate it before you board and you'll have data the moment you land. Popular options: Airalo or Maya apps. Search "Korea data eSIM" — most 7–10 day plans are $10–20 USD. Most iPhones from XR onward support eSIM.

Prefer a physical SIM? Pick one up at KT, SK Telecom, or LG U+ kiosks right in the Incheon arrivals hall.

Airalo나 Maya 앱에서 eSIM을 미리 구매하거나 공항 도착 후 통신사 부스에서 SIM 카드를 구입하세요.

Get a T-Money card — it works on all subways, city buses, and even some taxis. Pick one up at any convenience store (GS25, CU, 7-Eleven, Ministop) or at subway station vending machines. The card costs about 3,000 KRW (~$2 USD). Load it with 30,000–50,000 KRW to start and top up at any convenience store counter.

Shortcut: Many iPhones and Android phones can add a virtual T-Money card to Apple Pay or Google Pay — check your wallet settings after you land.

T-Money 카드는 편의점(GS25, CU, 세븐일레븐 등)이나 지하철역에서 구입 가능합니다. 약 3,000원이며 충전식입니다.

Seoul's subway is world-class, clean, air-conditioned, and runs until about midnight. Tap in by touching your T-Money card to the yellow reader at the turnstile. Tap out when you exit — this is important, as skipping it charges you the maximum fare. Buses work the same way: tap on, tap off. Transfer between subway and bus within 30 minutes and the second ride is free or discounted.

All signs and announcements are in English too. Use Google Maps or Naver Maps for directions — both work great in Seoul.

승하차 시 T-Money 카드를 단말기에 태그하세요. 30분 이내 환승 시 추가 요금 없음.

Uber is not widely used in Korea. Download Kakao Taxi instead — it's the Korean equivalent, works great, is in English, and accepts credit cards. Download it before you arrive. You can also hail street taxis — just show the driver your destination in Korean text (copy it from Google Maps). Fares are very reasonable: a 20-minute cross-city ride is roughly $8–12 USD.

카카오택시 앱을 미리 설치하세요. 일반 택시도 많으며 카드와 현금 모두 사용 가능합니다.

Currency is Korean Won (KRW). Roughly 1,300–1,400 KRW = $1 USD, so 10,000 KRW ≈ $7–8. Visa and Mastercard are accepted almost everywhere. Carry some cash for street food stalls.

Best way to get cash: Use a no-foreign-fee debit card (Charles Schwab, Wise) at a local ATM. The Wowpass card is also popular — load it with KRW at the airport and use it like a debit card everywhere.

한국 화폐는 원(KRW)입니다. 신용카드는 대부분의 곳에서 사용 가능합니다.

Formal or semi-formal attire. Cocktail dresses, suits, or traditional Korean hanbok are all warmly welcome. Please avoid wearing white or ivory out of respect for the bride.

포멀 또는 세미포멀 복장. 칵테일 드레스, 정장, 또는 한복 모두 환영합니다. 신부를 위해 흰색이나 아이보리는 피해주세요.

Due to venue capacity, please only bring guests named on your invitation — indicate your plus one in your RSVP if included. Children are welcome at the ceremony. For the evening reception, we recommend arranging childcare if possible so the adults can fully enjoy the celebration.

초대장에 명시된 분들만 참석 가능합니다. 어린이는 결혼식에 환영하며, 저녁 피로연은 보육 서비스 이용을 권장합니다.

September is one of the best times to visit Seoul. Expect warm, pleasant days around 22–26°C (72–79°F) with cool evenings. The summer humidity has passed and autumn colors are just beginning. Light layers are ideal — a cardigan or light jacket for the evening is recommended. Rain is possible but not common; a small umbrella or packable rain jacket is worth bringing.

9월은 서울을 방문하기 가장 좋은 시기 중 하나입니다. 낮에는 22~26°C의 따뜻하고 쾌적한 날씨, 저녁에는 선선합니다. 가벼운 겉옷을 준비하시고 우산도 챙기시면 좋습니다.

Yes — please note any dietary restrictions in your RSVP and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Korean cuisine is wonderfully varied and there will be something for everyone.

For any other questions, feel free to reach out to us directly — contact details coming soon.

RSVP에 식이 제한 사항을 기재해 주세요. 다른 문의 사항은 직접 연락주시면 됩니다 — 연락처 추후 안내.
